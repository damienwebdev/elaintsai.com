Homepage Redesign
Simplified Navigation and Banner Displays
Lost Golf Balls customers want to be able to easily access promotions and products, and the overall marketing goal is to generate higher conversion rates. The 2016 iteration of the home page was cluttered and hard to navigate. Additionally, as mobile use increases, the website failed in providing a smooth mobile shopping experience for consumers.
Navigation
Restructuring the navigation bar was the top prority since it would fix a large portion of the usability and mobile issues on the website. Initially, the main navigation bar was cluttered with links that were not frequently visited, and the category pages were pushed down to the sidebar.
As an ecommerce website, the main objective is to properly advertise sales and promotions to entice consumers to make a purchase.
