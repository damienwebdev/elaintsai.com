The Process
Transformative Design
Traceable.com is an ecommerce website built with atomic design principles and implemented through agile methology to produce a product that is intentional and transformative.

By being agile in the design process, progressive improvements can be made. Designs will never be perfect the first time around, so it is invaluable to have constant client and consumer interaction and feedback that allow for rapid revision and implementation, resulting in a better end product.

The Traceable Products interface is essentially made up of small components that can be reused throughout the website. By using atomic design principles and designing with the style guide as a priority, it creates a challenge to stay organized and retain consistency across the entire website experience.
